Rules

• Any issues/exploits with challenges/infra should be reported immediately. Join our discord or send an email to us (details below in the footer)

• Don't share flags or collaborate with other teams in any way. If someone asks you for flags or hints, please report them to organisers.

• Each player can only be on one team.

• Please be respectful of our shared infrastructure — don't try to attack our servers, DDoS anything, or run automated scanners against challenges.

• The flag format regex is /gaslightCTF{[\w\-_!?]+}/. For example: gaslightCTF{w3lc0me_2_g4sl1ghtCTF!}

• Online resources and tools are fair game. Automated scanners/solvers like sqlmap are not, you won't need them anyway.

• No LLMs or AI assistants for solving challenges in any way. LLM-assisted code completion in your IDE is fine, but anything more than that is not (if in doubt, ask!). gaslightCTF is about learning, and research shows AI assistance can get in the way of actually building those skills.
