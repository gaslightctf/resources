What is gaslightCTF?

gaslightCTF is a capture the flag competition targeted mainly towards beginners / UK secondary school students. But everyone is welcome.

We aim to help the younger generation develop their programming and problem solving skills.

Extra Info

gaslightCTF is developed by two aspiring UK students.

After participating in and realizing most CTF (capture the flag) competitions were based outside the UK, excluding UK students from things such as student prize pools and student leaderboards. We decided to take action and start our own. Letting students from all around the UK learn and compete for prizes that are in reach.

We aim to be a CTF similar to hyperlink:picoCTF, a CTF also maily geared towards beginners, gamifying education, helping the younger generation learn about cybersecurity.
