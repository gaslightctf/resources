What is gaslightCTF?

gaslightCTF is a jeopardy-style capture the flag competition open to everyone — from complete beginners to more advanced players. Teams of up to five can compete across three divisions: Secondary School, University, and Open.

We aim to bring cybersecurity education outside of the US and to the wider world.

Extra Info

gaslightCTF is developed by a team of UK secondary school students.

After participating in CTFs and realising most competitions were based in the US — excluding international students from student prize pools and leaderboards — we decided to take action and start our own, letting students from all around the world learn and compete for prizes that are in reach.

We aim to be a CTF similar to picoCTF, also mainly geared towards beginners, gamifying education and helping the younger generation learn about cybersecurity.

New to CTFs? Check out the picoCTF primer for a beginner's guide!
