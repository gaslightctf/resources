About

We started playing CTFs in 2024, starting (as many other secondary schooler CTFers do) with picoCTF - a CTF competition hosted by Carnegie Mellon University targeted mainly towards high school students in the US. As a beginner competition with lots of learning resources, picoCTF got us into the world of CTFs, and since then we’ve participated in dozens of CTFs, some of which are shown on our team’s CTFTime profile

However as we got into more and more CTFs, we noticed that many were targeted towards students in the US, and prize pools for students (when available) were usually limited to US students. This gap can also be seen on CTFTime - as of 2026-05-11, there are 6 ranked UK teams in contrast to 76 in the US.

That’s why we decided this year to host our very own CTF competition, targeting beginner secondary students both in the UK and around the world as well. We think that in an increasingly digital world, cybersecurity is an important (and also very fun!) pathway that we hope to introduce more students like us to.
